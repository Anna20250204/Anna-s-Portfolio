Postmodern Typography Poster

Paper, Photoshop / W 10inch x H 38inch

This project is about creating a poster for a fictional lecture on typography with three invited speakers. My subject was Postmodern Typography, and three famous female artists, Barbara Kruger, Jenny Holzer, and Paula Shea, were invited. The poster information was written using the Futura typeface, which Barbra Kruger and Jenny Holzer mainly use. While making this poster, the essential point for me was to create a poster that fits the theme of Modern typography.

Process

The process of the poster's background was created using Photoshop bitmap after scanning the printed word ‘Typography’ in various ways. By having a huge contrast between background and type gives a Postmodernism look to my poster.
